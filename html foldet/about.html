About us



Enzzyme capital partner is a team of expert who manage Africa-focused Venture funds & Venture growth programs.we have empowered over 100 organization across 16 industries/verticals,shaping the future and leaving a lasting impact.

Our structure

Enzzyme Capital Partners is built on a strategic alliance of African fund managers and innovators. Our team of fund managers, investment professionals, builders, operators and strategic partners (supported by our Advisory Board and Investment Committee) utilize our vast local network covering all geopolitical zones and key markets for impactful venture investing. Our fund operations are further enhanced by our international presence and networks in the USA, Kenya, Dubai, and the UK

Global Presence

Enzzyme Capital Partners has a footprint across major cities in Nigeria and extends its reach through a last-mile field operations network covering 98% of local government areas in Nigeria. From Unguwar Shanu (Kaduna) to Wuse 2 (Abuja), Ikeja (Lagos), and international offices in Texas (US), Nairobi (KE), Dubai (UAE), and London (UK).

Enzzyme Capital Partners, a fund manager with 50+ years of collective VC experience, has propelled over 100 organizations, weaving innovation into Africa's business fabric, and managing $20M+ in VC Funds. Join us in shaping the future.
